Our Story

People Partner AI was founded in 2023 by a team of HR professionals and technology experts who recognized a critical gap in the market. Small and medium-sized businesses were struggling to access affordable, reliable HR support, while larger organizations faced inefficiencies in their HR processes.

We saw an opportunity to leverage artificial intelligence to bridge this gap, creating a platform that combines the expertise of seasoned HR professionals with the scalability and accessibility of modern technology.

Today, we serve thousands of businesses across the UK and beyond, helping them navigate complex employment legislation, streamline HR processes, and create better workplaces for their teams.
